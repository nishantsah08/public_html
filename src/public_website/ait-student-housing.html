Booking & Onboarding Checklist

Complete the following steps to confirm your bed before the new term begins:

  1. Share your AIT admission/bonafide letter and government ID over WhatsApp for provisional blocking.
  2. Join a guided video call tour of the rooms, washrooms, and common areas with the caretaker.
  3. Pay the security deposit to lock the bed and schedule your move-in (up to 30 days in advance).
  4. Submit a signed stay agreement and recent passport photo on the day of arrival.

AIT Student FAQs

Can I schedule a room tour if I am currently on campus?

Yes. Most cadets take a live video walk-through with the caretaker. If you prefer an in-person visit, the property is open for tours every Saturday between 11:00 AM and 4:00 PM.

Are guests allowed inside the premises?

Parents and immediate family members can visit between 9:00 AM and 7:00 PM after notifying the caretaker. Overnight stays by non-residents are not permitted to keep the environment secure.

What happens if my internship or camp requires me to stay away for a month?

You can retain your bed by paying the rent for the period you are away. We can also list the bed for temporary subletting to an AIT batchmate if you provide at least two weeks' notice.
