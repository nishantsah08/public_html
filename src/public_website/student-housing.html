Well-Connected to Pune’s Education Belt

  • AIT, D. Y. Patil, MIT Alandi: Reach these campuses in 8–25 minutes by walk, PMPML buses, or shared auto from the Dighi Gaon stand.
  • PMPML coverage: Daily bus services 171, 119, and 135 link Dighi with Vishrantwadi, Shivajinagar, and the Pune railway station.
  • Everyday essentials on foot: Groceries, stationery, print shops, and medical stores are within a 5-minute walk.
  • Healthcare nearby: Columbia Asia Hospital and military dispensaries are less than 3 km away for emergency support.

Student Support Services

We go beyond basic facilities to maintain a productive, community-first environment for students from different institutes.

  • Dual broadband lines: 200 Mbps fibre with a 4G failover ensures uninterrupted online classes and submissions.
  • Quiet hours & mentoring: Noise-free slots from 9:30 PM and bi-monthly peer study clubs organised by senior residents.

How to Reserve Your Bed

Admissions stay open year-round with limited fast-filling beds during July and December intakes.

  1. Request the latest availability chart over WhatsApp and pick your preferred sharing option.
  2. Complete a virtual or physical property tour along with your guardian.
  3. Submit identity proof and college ID, then transfer the refundable security deposit.
  4. Sign the digital stay agreement and schedule move-in assistance with our caretaker.

Student Housing FAQs

Do you accommodate both male and female students?

The current building is reserved for male students. We maintain a waiting list for female students and will notify you as soon as the second property opens.

Can I move in mid-semester?

Yes. Pro-rated rent adjustments are reflected in the second month’s invoice. Beds are allocated on a first-confirmed basis.

Is there storage for projects or lab equipment?

Each floor has a lockable utility cabinet that residents can use for models, drawing boards, or lab kits after informing the caretaker.
