TCL Employee FAQs

Is meal service available for early morning or late-night shifts?

We collaborate with caterers who deliver breakfast from 6:30 AM and late dinners till 11:00 PM. A kitchenette with microwave and induction stove is accessible round-the-clock.

Can I host a colleague for short visits?

Yes, colleagues can visit between 8:00 AM and 9:00 PM after signing the visitor register. Overnight stays require prior approval and are limited to immediate family.

Do you support temporary stays during training weeks?

We maintain two flexible beds for 2–4 week training assignments. Reach out to the caretaker at least a week in advance to block the slot.
